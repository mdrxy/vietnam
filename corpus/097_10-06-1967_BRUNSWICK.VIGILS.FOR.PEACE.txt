@TITLE=Brunswick Vigils for Peace@BODY=The Sunday Vigils for Peace, begun lastspring, will continue this year. Interested studentsand local citizens will assemble at the Mallin the center of Brunswick at noon and standsilently until 12:30.
The Sunday Peace Vigils are not sponsoredby any political or religious organization. Theyrepresent local concern for the loss of human lifeand the destruction of property in North andSouth Vietnam.
